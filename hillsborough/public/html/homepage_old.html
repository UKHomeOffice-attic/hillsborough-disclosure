With 96 lives lost, the Hillsborough disaster of 1989 is the most serious tragedy at a British sports event. 23 years on, the Hillsborough Independent Panel has disclosed over 400,000 pages from 30,000 documents, many for the first time, and published its Report explaining how this adds to public understanding.
